CodeBox

 
CodeBox is a user-friendly snippet manager which changes the way you organize all your code snippets, and provides a seamless synchronization across Windows, Linux and Mac.
 
 
 

Synchronization

By enabling sync you can access all your snippets from enywhere, you can also share a given snippet and others can share snippets woth you too, which you can edit and keep up with the updates.

Backup

By enabling sync you can access all your snippets are uploaded to our servers, but you also have the option to export all your snippets and keep updating with new ones automaticlly. If you choose to export it to a cloud folder you can easily keep your phones and tablets updates too. You can also export by queries.

Customizability

You can choose from multiple themes separately for the editor and the general interface. You can set the editor font size, line-height, font-family and a lot more.

Code Annotation

Real time code annotation is avaiable for more then 60 languages, including:

APL, C, C#, C++, Clojure, Cobol, CoffeeScript, Common Lisp, Crystal, CSS, Cypher, D, Dart, Django, DTD, Eiffel, Elm, Erlang, Fortran, Go, Groovy, Haxe, HTML, HTTP, Jade, JAVA, Jade, JavaScript, Julia, Less, LiveScript, Lua, Mathematica, Modelica, Nginx, Objective C, Octave, Pascal, Perl, Pig, PowerShell, Puppet, Python, Q, R, Ruby, Shell, Sass, Scala, Scheme, SmallTalk, Spreadsheet, SQL, Stylus, Swift, VB, VBScript, XQuery, Yacas

Sharing

You can create groups and share snippets with all group members, who can edit them and keep you updated about new fragments, you can also share snippets with only a friend.

FAQ

How to synchronize with a mobile or a tablet

At the moment CodeBox is not yet availbale for mobiles and tablets, but you can set up a one-way synchronization by exporting all snippets into a cloud folder and sync that folder with your mobiles and tablets

    Sync Clients:
  • iOS
  • Android

How to turn on synchronization

You can enable synchronization in settings, click on the caret next to your username, then settings, then toggle synchronization, and your data will be uploaded and updated

How to add snippets, tags, friends and groups

You can add them by clicking on the circular buttons in the sidebar

How many fragments can I store inside a snippet

You can store unlimited ammount of fragments and set the cache size for each device separately

How can I share a snippet

You can share a snippet with just a friend or a group, just click on share and select the intended recipients, after sharing everybody you shared it with will be able to see all the fragments you added and also add new ones, but they will only be able to delete their own fragments.

Are you planning to release an app for mobiles

If there is a demand I will develope the app for iOS and Andoid too.
